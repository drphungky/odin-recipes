Lasagna For One

Description

A lovely, entirely homemade traditional pasta dish, and definitely enough to feed you. Don't worry about labels.

Ingredients

  • Credit Card
  • Wine (Optional)

Steps

  1. Go to Store
  2. Buy a family size Stouffer's lasagna. In the checkout line, mention loudly that it will definitely feed your large family, and not just you.
  3. Return home, cook lasagna according to package
  4. Eat entire lasagna
  5. (Optional) Cry quietly into your wine
